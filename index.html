About me / 01

I'm a Market & Corporate Finance student at Kedge Business School, passionate about the intersection of quantitative research, capital markets, and technology.

My work spans equity research, strategic asset allocation for portfolio construction, academic econometrics papers, and full-stack data projects. I believe rigorous analysis and clear communication are equally important.

When not deep in spreadsheets or terminals, I write about macro trends, contribute to open-source financial tools, and explore how AI is reshaping investment workflows.
